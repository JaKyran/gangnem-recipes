Spicy Korean Tofu Soup


Description

Spicy Korean Tofu Soup, also known as Sundubu-jjigae, is a comforting and flavorful dish made with soft tofu, vegetables, and a spicy broth. It's a popular Korean dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or side dish.

'

Ingredients

  • Soft Tofu: This is the star ingredient of the dish. It gives the soup its creamy texture.
  • Vegetables: Common vegetables include zucchini, mushrooms, and green onions.
  • Kimchi: Adds a tangy and spicy flavor to the soup.
  • Gochugaru: Korean red pepper flakes that give the soup its signature spiciness.
  • Broth: You can use vegetable or chicken broth as a base for the soup.
  • Egg: A beaten egg is often added to the soup just before serving for extra richness.

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat some oil and add the gochugaru and kimchi. Cook for a few minutes.
  2. Add the broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the vegetables and soft tofu. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Just before serving, crack an egg into the soup and let it cook for a minute.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
